Raccoon

Raccoons can open complex locks in under 10 attempts!

  • Scientific Name: Procyon lotor
  • Average Length: 60-95 cm
  • Average Weight: 5-15 kg
  • Lifespan: 2-3 years in the wild

Common Raccoons are stocky animals with short legs and small, rounded ears. Their fur is gray, with dark black markings around their eyes, and black bands on their tail. Their belly and muzzle are lighter colored while the feet are darker gray. Raccoons have coarse, medium-length fur and a very bushy tail.
